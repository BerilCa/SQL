About
This project aims to explore data on terrorist attacks from 1970-2023. The goal is to find out what methods are most used by terrorists for their actions, in order to overcome the possibility of future attacks.

Purposes Of The Project
The purpose of this analysis is to find out the attack methods that are often used by terrorists for their actions, in order to get an overview of anticipation in a country that has terrorist issues.
